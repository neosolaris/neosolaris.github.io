Vim Startify

2022-08-23 · (updated 2022-08-26) · 59 words

Install#

Plug 'mhinz/vim-startify'

Key Binding#

" `SPC l s` - save current session
nnoremap <leader>ls :SSave<CR>

" `SPC l l` - list sessions / switch to different project
nnoremap <leader>ll :SClose<CR>
